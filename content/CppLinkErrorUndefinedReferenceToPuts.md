Solution
--------

 

Change the linker from 'arm-eabi-ld' to 'arm-eabi-gcc' in the [Qt
project file](CppQtProjectFile.htm):

 

  -----------------------------------------------------------
  ` QMAKE_LINK = /opt/devkitpro/devkitARM/bin/arm-eabi-gcc`
  -----------------------------------------------------------
