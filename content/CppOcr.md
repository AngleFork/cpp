([C++](Cpp.htm)) [OCR](CppOcr.htm)
==================================

 

[OCR](CppOcr.htm) (an abbreviation of 'Optical Character Recognition')
is the technique to let an [algorithm](CppAlgorithm.htm) determine the
characters from a hand-written image.
