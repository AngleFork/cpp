=================================================================================================================

 

The [Abstract Factory](CppDesignPatternAbstractFactory.md) is a [Design
Pattern](CppDesignPattern.md) 'to provide an interface for creating
families of related or dependent objects without specifying their
concrete classes' \[1\]
