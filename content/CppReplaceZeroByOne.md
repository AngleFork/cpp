[Code snippet](CppCodeSnippets.md) to replace all zeroes in a
[std::vector](CppStdVector.md) by a one.

 

  ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  ` #include <vector> #include <algorithm  void ReplaceZeroByOne(std::vector<int>& v) {   std::replace(v.begin(),v.end(),0,1);  }`
