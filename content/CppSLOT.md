====================================

 

[SLOT](CppSLOT.htm) is a [Qt](CppQt.htm) [macro](CppMacro.htm) that
basically converts a [slot](CppSlot.htm) to a string.

 

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  ` QObject::connect(   my_sender,   SIGNAL(my_signal()),   my_reveiver,   SLOT(my_slot()));`
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
