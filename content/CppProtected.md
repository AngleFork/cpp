==============================================

 

[protected](CppProtected.htm) is a [keyword](CppKeyword.htm) to a
[class](CppClass.htm) its [access level](CppAccessLevel.htm) to
[protected](CppProtected.htm) or for [protected
inheritance](CppProtectedInheritance.htm).

 

 

 

 

 

Examples
--------

 

-   [protected example 1: a protected member
    variable](CppProtectedExample1.htm) (but note \[2\])

 

 

 

 

 

[Advice](CppAdvice.htm)
-----------------------

 

-   Use [protected](CppProtected.htm) [members](CppMember.htm) only
    carefully when really needed \[1\]
-   Don't [declare](CppDeclaration.htm) [member
    variables](CppMemberVariable.htm) [protected](CppProtected.htm)
    \[2\]
