[WtDialWidget](CppWtDialWidget.htm) is a [Wt](CppWt.htm)
[widget](CppWidget.htm) [class](CppClass.htm) to display an
[DialWidget](CppDialWidget.htm).

 

[WtDialWidget](CppWtDialWidget.htm) is used in the [tool](Tools.htm)
[TestDial](ToolTestDial.htm).
