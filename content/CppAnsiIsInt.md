==============================================

 

[AnsiIsInt](CppAnsiIsInt.htm) is a [check](CppCheck.htm) [code
snippet](CppCodeSnippets.htm) to see if an
[AnsiString](CppAnsiString.htm) can be [converted](CppConvert.htm) to an
[integer](CppInt.htm).
