======================================

 

A [graph](CppGraph.htm) (also called a network) is a connection of nodes
connected by edges.

 

[Boost.Graph](CppBoostGraph.htm) is a [Boost](CppBoost.htm)
[library](CppLibrary.htm) for [graphs](CppGraph.htm).
