======================================

 

A [graph](CppGraph.md) (also called a network) is a connection of nodes
connected by edges.

 

[Boost.Graph](CppBoostGraph.md) is a [Boost](CppBoost.md)
[library](CppLibrary.md) for [graphs](CppGraph.md).
