==================================================================================================

 

The [Mediator](CppDesignPatternMediator.md) is a [Design
Pattern](CppDesignPattern.md) to 'define an object that encapsulates
how a set of objects interact. Mediator promotes looses coupling by
keeping objects from referring to each other explicitly, and it lets you
vary their interactions independently' \[1\]
