([C++](Cpp.htm)) [std::begin](CppStdBegin.htm)
==============================================

 

[std::begin](CppStdBegin.htm) is a [C++11](Cpp11.htm)
[function](CppFunction.htm) to obtain an [iterator](CppIterator.htm) to
the beginning of a container.
