([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Keyword](CppKeyword.htm)
==========================================

 

Keywords are the words that 'do' something. These words therefore cannot
be used as variable names. In many C++ [IDE](CppIde.htm)'s, these appear
in **bold**.

 

  -------------------------
  ` int if = 3; //ERROR!`
  -------------------------
