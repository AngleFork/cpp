==========================================

 

Keywords are the words that 'do' something. These words therefore cannot
be used as variable names. In many C++ [IDE](CppIde.md)'s, these appear
in **bold**.

 

  -------------------------
  ` int if = 3; //ERROR!`
  -------------------------
