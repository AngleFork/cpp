================================================================

 

[clock.h](CppClockH.md) is a standard [header file](CppHeaderFile.md)
(since the [C++11](Cpp11.md) [standard](CppStandard.md)).

 

Prefer [clock.h](CppClockH.md) facilities over [ctime.h](CppCtimeH.md)
facilities \[1\].
