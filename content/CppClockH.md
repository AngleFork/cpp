================================================================

 

[clock.h](CppClockH.htm) is a standard [header file](CppHeaderFile.htm)
(since the [C++11](Cpp11.htm) [standard](CppStandard.htm)).

 

Prefer [clock.h](CppClockH.htm) facilities over [ctime.h](CppCtimeH.htm)
facilities \[1\].
