([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Margaret A. Ellis](CppMargaretAEllis.htm)
===========================================================

 

[Margaret A. Ellis](CppMargaretAEllis.htm) is the co-author (with
[Bjarne Stroustrup](CppBjarneStroustrup.htm)) of 'The Annotated C++
Reference Manual'.
