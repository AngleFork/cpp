===========================================

 

[locale.h](CppLocaleH.htm) is a C [header file](CppHeaderFile.htm) for
localization. [clocale.h](CppClocaleH.htm) is its [STL](CppStl.htm)
equivalent.

 

  -----------------------
  ` #include <clocale>`
