([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Interpreter](CppDesignPatternInterpreter.htm) ([Design Pattern](CppDesignPattern.htm))
========================================================================================================

 

The [Interpreter](CppDesignPatternInterpreter.htm) is a [Design
Pattern](CppDesignPattern.htm) for 'given a language, define a
representation for its grammar along with an interpreter that uses the
representation to interpret sequences in the language' \[1\]
