========================================

 

[TChart](CppTChart.md) is a [VCL data type](CppVclDataType.md) for a
chart.

 

[TChart](CppTChart.md) cannot do a surface plot. For a surface plot
function, [go to the SurfacePlotter page](CppSurfacePlotterTImage.md).
