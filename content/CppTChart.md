========================================

 

[TChart](CppTChart.htm) is a [VCL data type](CppVclDataType.htm) for a
chart.

 

[TChart](CppTChart.htm) cannot do a surface plot. For a surface plot
function, [go to the SurfacePlotter page](CppSurfacePlotterTImage.htm).
