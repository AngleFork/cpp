[How to draw a sprite on a sprite?](CppQtSpriteOnSprite.htm) is a [Qt
FAQ](CppQtFaq.htm) when you want to stretchdraw differently scaled
images on top of each other, like [this image](CppQtSpriteOnSprite.png).
