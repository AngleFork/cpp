====================================================================================================

 

The [Composite](CppDesignPatternComposite.md) is a [Design
Pattern](CppDesignPattern.md) to 'compose objects into tree structures
to represent part-whole hierarchies. Composites lets clients treat
individual objects and composition of objects uniformly' \[1\]
