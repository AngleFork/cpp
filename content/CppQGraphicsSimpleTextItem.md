===========================================================================================

 

[QGraphicsSimpleTextItem](CppQGraphicsSimpleTextItem.htm) is a
[QGraphicsItem](CppQGraphicsItem.htm) for displaying a text in a
[QGraphicsScene](CppQGraphicsScene.htm).
