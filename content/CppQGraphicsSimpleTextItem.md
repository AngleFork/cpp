===========================================================================================

 

[QGraphicsSimpleTextItem](CppQGraphicsSimpleTextItem.md) is a
[QGraphicsItem](CppQGraphicsItem.md) for displaying a text in a
[QGraphicsScene](CppQGraphicsScene.md).
