[WtAboutDialog](CppWtAboutDialog.md) is a [class](CppClass.md) for a
[Wt](CppWt.md) dialog displaying the [About](CppAbout.md)
[class](CppClass.md).

 

[WtAboutDialog](CppWtAboutDialog.md) is used in nearly all my web
applications.
