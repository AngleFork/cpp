========================================================================================

 

A [mobile application](CppMobileApplication.htm) is a type of
[application](CppApplication.htm) that must be installed and executed on
a mobile phone.

-   ![Symbian](PicSymbian.png) [Symbian](CppSymbian.htm): supports
    [Qt](CppQt.htm)
