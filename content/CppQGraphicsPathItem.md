===============================================================================

 

[QGraphicsPathItem](CppQGraphicsPathItem.htm) is a
[QGraphicsItem](CppQGraphicsItem.htm) for displaying a
[QPainterPath](CpQPainterPath.htm) in a
[QGraphicsScene](CppQGraphicsScene.htm).
