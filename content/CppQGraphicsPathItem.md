===============================================================================

 

[QGraphicsPathItem](CppQGraphicsPathItem.md) is a
[QGraphicsItem](CppQGraphicsItem.md) for displaying a
[QPainterPath](CpQPainterPath.md) in a
[QGraphicsScene](CppQGraphicsScene.md).
