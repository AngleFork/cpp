([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Predicate](CppPredicate.htm)
==============================================

 

A [predicate](CppPredicate.htm) is a logical expression that evaluates
to [true](CppTrue.htm) or [false](CppFalse.htm), like in
[if](CppIf.htm)-statements.
