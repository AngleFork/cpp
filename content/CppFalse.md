======================================

 

[false](CppFalse.htm) is a [keyword](CppKeyword.htm) and one of the two
posible values of the [bool](CppBool.htm) [data type](CppDataType.htm),
the other value being [true](CppTrue.htm).

 

  ----------------------------------
  ` const bool something = false;`
  ----------------------------------
