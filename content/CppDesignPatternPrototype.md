([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Prototype](CppDesignPatternPrototype.htm) ([Design Pattern](CppDesignPattern.htm))
====================================================================================================

 

The [Prototype](CppDesignPatternPrototype.htm) is a [Design
Pattern](CppDesignPattern.htm) to 'specifiy the kinds of objects to
create using a prototypical instance, and create new objects by copying
this prototype' \[1\]
