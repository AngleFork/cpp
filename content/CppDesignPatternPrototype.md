====================================================================================================

 

The [Prototype](CppDesignPatternPrototype.md) is a [Design
Pattern](CppDesignPattern.md) to 'specifiy the kinds of objects to
create using a prototypical instance, and create new objects by copying
this prototype' \[1\]
