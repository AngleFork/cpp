[MXE example 3: Hello Qt](CppMxeExample3.md) is an [MXE](CppMxe.md)
[example](CppExample.md) to [cross-compile](CppCrossCompile.md) a
[Hello Qt](CppHelloQt.md) program from [GNU/Linux](CppLinux.md) to
[Windows](CppWindows.md).

    'CppMxeExample3' (zip)](CppMxeExample3.zip)
