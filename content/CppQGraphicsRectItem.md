===============================================================================

 

[QGraphicsRectItem](CppQGraphicsRectItem.htm) is a
[QGraphicsItem](CppQGraphicsItem.htm) for displaying a rectangle in a
[QGraphicsScene](CppQGraphicsScene.htm).
