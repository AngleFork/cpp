([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Herb Sutter](CppHerbSutter.htm)
=================================================

 

One of the [most important C++ people](CppMostImportantCppPeople.htm)
\[1\] and author. Host of 'Guru of the Week', a website with many
programming questions.

 

List of books (co-)authored by [Herb Sutter](CppHerbSutter.htm)
---------------------------------------------------------------

 

-   [Herb Sutter](CppHerbSutter.htm), [Andrei
    Alexandrescu](CppAndreiAlexandrescu.htm). C++ coding standards: 101
    rules, guidelines, and best practices. 2005. ISBN: 0-32-111358-6.
-   [Herb Sutter](CppHerbSutter.htm). Exceptional C++.
    ISBN: 0-201-61562-2.
-   [Herb Sutter](CppHerbSutter.htm). Exceptional C++ style. 2005.
    ISBN: 0-201-76042-8.
-   [Herb Sutter](CppHerbSutter.htm). More exceptional C++. 2001.
    ISBN: 020170434X.
