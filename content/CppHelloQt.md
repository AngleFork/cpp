============================================================

 

[Hello Qt](CppHelloQt.md) is an extension of [Hello
World](CppHelloWorld.md). Like [Hello World](CppHelloWorld.md), [Hello
Qt](CppHelloQt.md) is a very simple application. [Hello
Qt](CppHelloQt.md), however, also requires the [Qt](CppQt.md)
[libraries](CppLibrary.md).
