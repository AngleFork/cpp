([C++](Cpp.htm)) [do](CppDo.htm)
================================

 

[do](CppDo.htm) is a [keyword](CppKeyword.htm) to start a
[while](CppWhile.htm)-loop that is evaluated at least once.

 

Avoid do-statements \[1-2\].
