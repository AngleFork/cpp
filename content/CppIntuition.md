==============================================

 

[Intuition](CppIntuition.md) is a feeling about what is wrong or right,
slow or fast.

 

 

 

 

 

[Advice](CppAdvice.md)
-----------------------

 

-   When it comes to [performance](CppPerformance.md), don't trust your
    [intuition](CppIntuition.md): measure \[1\]
