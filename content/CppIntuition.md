==============================================

 

[Intuition](CppIntuition.htm) is a feeling about what is wrong or right,
slow or fast.

 

 

 

 

 

[Advice](CppAdvice.htm)
-----------------------

 

-   When it comes to [performance](CppPerformance.htm), don't trust your
    [intuition](CppIntuition.htm): measure \[1\]
