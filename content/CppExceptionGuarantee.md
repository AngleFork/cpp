=================================================================

 

There are three types of [exception
guarantee](CppExceptionGuarantee.md):

[basic guarantee](CppBasicGuarantee.md)

[strong guarantee](CppStrongGuarantee.md)

[nothrow guarantee](CppNothrowGuarantee.md)

 

Always provide the [basic guarantee](CppBasicGuarantee.md) \[1\].
Provide the [strong guarantee](CppStrongGuarantee.md) unless there is a
reason not to \[2\].
