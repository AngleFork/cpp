=================================================================

 

There are three types of [exception
guarantee](CppExceptionGuarantee.htm):

[basic guarantee](CppBasicGuarantee.htm)

[strong guarantee](CppStrongGuarantee.htm)

[nothrow guarantee](CppNothrowGuarantee.htm)

 

Always provide the [basic guarantee](CppBasicGuarantee.htm) \[1\].
Provide the [strong guarantee](CppStrongGuarantee.htm) unless there is a
reason not to \[2\].
