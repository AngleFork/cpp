=============================================

 

[link time](CppLinkTime.htm) is the the time it takes the
[linker](CppLinker.htm) to connect the [compiled](CppCompile.htm) object
code.

 

[link time](CppLinkTime.htm) is followed by [run time](CppRunTime.htm).
