=============================================

 

[void\*](CppVoidPointer.htm) (or [void pointer](CppVoidPointer.htm)) is
a type of [pointer](CppPointer.htm) to an [address](CppAddress.htm) of
unknown [data type](CppDataType.htm).

 

Avoid [void\*](CppVoidPointer.htm) except in low-level code \[1\].
