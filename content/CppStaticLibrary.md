=======================================================

 

A [static library](CppStaticLibrary.htm) is a type
[library](CppLibrary.htm).

 

To build a [static library](CppStaticLibrary.htm) add, the following
line to the [Qt](CppQt.htm) [project file](CppProjectFile.htm):

 

  ------------------------
  ` CONFIG += staticlib`
  ------------------------
