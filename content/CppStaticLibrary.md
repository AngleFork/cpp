=======================================================

 

A [static library](CppStaticLibrary.md) is a type
[library](CppLibrary.md).

 

To build a [static library](CppStaticLibrary.md) add, the following
line to the [Qt](CppQt.md) [project file](CppProjectFile.md):

 

  ------------------------
  ` CONFIG += staticlib`
  ------------------------
