([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Const correctness](CppConstCorrect.htm)
=========================================================

 

[Const correctness](CppConstCorrect.htm) denotes one uses
[const](CppConst.htm) whenever possible/feasible. Prefer to use
[const](CppConst.htm) whenever possible \[1-6\]. Be [const
correct](CppConstCorrect.htm) \[7-11\].
