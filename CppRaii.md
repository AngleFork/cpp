([C++](Cpp.htm)) [Resource Acquisition Is Initialization](CppRaii.htm)
======================================================================

 

The [Resource Acquisition Is Initialization](CppRaii.htm) (abbreviated
to '[RAII](CppRaii.htm)') is a technique.

 

 

 

 

 

[Advice](CppAdvice.htm)
-----------------------

 

-   Use [RAII](CppRaii.htm) to manage [resources](CppResource.htm)
    \[1,3\]
-   Use [RAII](CppRaii.htm) and exception handlers to maintain
    [invariants](CppInvariant.htm) \[2\]
