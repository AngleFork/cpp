([C++](Cpp.htm)) [string literal](CppStringLiteral.htm)
=======================================================

 

A [string literal](CppStringLiteral.htm) is a character sequence
enclosed with double quotes \[1\]. A single character enclosed in single
quotes is called a [character literal](CppCharacterLiteral.htm) \[2\].
Use [raw strings](CppRawString.htm) for [string
literals](CppStringLiteral.htm.htm) with complicated uses of backslash
\[3\].
